Roti Recipe

Description

Fluffy and buttery flatbread served with saucy meals.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups flour
  • 4 tbsp of oil
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 cups (500ml) warm water
  • 60g butter

    • Steps

    1. In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients except the butter until soft dough is formed. Transfer to a floured surface and work the dough until its smooth. Place under a tea towel and allow the dough to rest and cool for 10 minutes.
    2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a long rectangular shape. Spread the butter over the entire surface of the dough and roll up the dough to form a long log. Cut into 4-5 cm pieces. Turn the visible rolls facing up. Give one gentle press with your hand on each piece and transfer to a floured platter.
    3. Cover with cling wrap and chill in the fridge for 1 hour.
    4. Roll out the roti balls into a round disc on a lightly oiled surface. Fry in a non-stick pan until golden brown on both sides.
